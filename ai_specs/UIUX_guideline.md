## 2. Typography
Fonts must be consistently applied to maintain readability across TMS/CRM platforms.

- [cite_start]**Headings:** Work Sans Semibold (Weight: 600)[cite: 22].
- [cite_start]**Body Text:** Open Sans (Standard Weight)[cite: 23].
- [cite_start]**Button Text:** Work Sans Semibold (Weight: 600), White color[cite: 24, 26].

## 3. Component Standards: Buttons
Buttons represent critical actions in the 24/7 supply chain workflow.

### 3.1 Primary Button (Orange Gradient)
- [cite_start]**Background:** Orange Gradient (Light at top, slightly darker at bottom)[cite: 25].
- [cite_start]**Text:** White, Work Sans Semibold[cite: 26].
- [cite_start]**Corner Radius:** Subtle radius applied **only to the bottom-right corner**[cite: 26].

### 3.2 Secondary/Dual Button Logic
- [cite_start]**Two Buttons Adjacent:** - 1st Button: Orange gradient background[cite: 28].
    - [cite_start]2nd Button: Outline style (Orange border)[cite: 28].
    - [cite_start]**Note:** Only the 2nd button receives the bottom-right corner radius[cite: 29].
- [cite_start]**Orange Background Context:** Use a white outline button for contrast[cite: 27].

## 4. Iconography
- [cite_start]**Style:** Lined style[cite: 11].
- [cite_start]**Line Color:** Brand Navy Blue (#011837)[cite: 12].
- [cite_start]**Accent Color:** Brand Cyan (#6BFFFF) as the prime accent color[cite: 11, 16].
- [cite_start]**Background Contrast:** When icons are used on a Gray White (#E7EEF1) background, use white background plates for contrast.

## 5. Implementation Rules
- Always prioritize **24/7 readability**.
- [cite_start]Follow the "Anti-Example" avoidance (ensure no color clashing or incorrect font weights)[cite: 19].
- [cite_start]Digital DNA: Design for visibility and control to empower supply chain management[cite: 21].
